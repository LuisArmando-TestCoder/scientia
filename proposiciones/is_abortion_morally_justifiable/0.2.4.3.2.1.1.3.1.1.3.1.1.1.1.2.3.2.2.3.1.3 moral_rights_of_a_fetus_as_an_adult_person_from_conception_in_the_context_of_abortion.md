## logical_tension

- **paradox**: To give the fetus the 'same' rights as an adult actually gives it 'more' rights than an adult, specifically the right to use another's organs to survive, which no adult can claim from another.
- **ambiguity**: The term 'person' shifts between biological ('human being') and moral ('member of the moral community').
- **useful_contradiction**: The fetus is simultaneously 'part of' the parent and 'separate from' the parent, a biological entanglement that exceeds standard individualist logic.
