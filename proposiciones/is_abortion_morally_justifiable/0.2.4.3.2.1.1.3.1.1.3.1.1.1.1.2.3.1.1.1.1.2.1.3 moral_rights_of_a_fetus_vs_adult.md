## argument_reasons

- Biological continuity argument: there is no non-arbitrary point between conception and adulthood to assign rights.
- Potentiality argument: a fetus possesses the inherent capacity to become a self-aware moral agent.
- Substance ontology: personhood is an essential nature, not a functional stage reached through development.

## reconclusion
