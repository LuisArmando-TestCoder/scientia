## conclusion

If we define life solely by the presence of a unique genetic blueprint, then personhood begins at conception; if we define it by consciousness or relationality, it begins later; and for all this, the question of abortion is a collision between an assigned ontological status and the physical reality of biological dependency. and for all this, the morality of abortion is remains a conflict of competing fundamental axioms regarding the hierarchy of rights.
