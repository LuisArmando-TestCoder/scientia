## central_semantic_node

Ontological and Moral Status of the Zygote/Fetus

## argument_reasons

- Biological continuity: The zygote is a genetically distinct, living human organism from the moment of fertilization.
- Potentiality: The early-stage organism has the inherent capacity to develop into a sentient adult human.
- Moral equivalence: If a fetus is a person, it possesses an inalienable right to life that supersedes bodily autonomy.
