## causal_chain

Biological life begins at fertilization creating a unique DNA sequence which defines the physical individual. This physical individual is categorized as a person within certain moral frameworks which demand that all persons possess equal intrinsic rights regardless of size or ability. Therefore, if the fetus is a person, it possesses a right to life equal to the adult. However, rights also involve the use of resources, and in this case, the resource is the adult's body, creating a conflict between two protected entities. Because the definition of 'person' is not found in the DNA but in the value assigned by the community, the logic loop returns to the starting point of value-assignment. This means the conclusion is always a reflection of the starting bias, making the chain a circle rather than a line.
