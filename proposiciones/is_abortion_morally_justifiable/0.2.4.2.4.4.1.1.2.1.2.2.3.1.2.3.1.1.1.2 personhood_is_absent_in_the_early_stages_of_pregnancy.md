## causal_chain

Morality governs interactions between subjects. Subjectivity is a product of personhood. Personhood requires a functional neurological architecture (a brain that works). Early pregnancy lacks a functional neurological architecture. Therefore, early pregnancy lacks personhood. Because it lacks personhood, it lacks subjectivity. Because it lacks subjectivity, there is no 'someone' to be harmed. Because there is no one to be harmed, the act of termination does not violate the moral rules of harming others. Therefore, abortion in early stages is justifiable because the conditions for moral violation are not met. The absence of the person-node collapses the barrier to justifiability.
