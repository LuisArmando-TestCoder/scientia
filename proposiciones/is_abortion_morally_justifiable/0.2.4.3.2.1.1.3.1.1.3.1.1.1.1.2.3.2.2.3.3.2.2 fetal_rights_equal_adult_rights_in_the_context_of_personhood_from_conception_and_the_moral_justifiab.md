## conclusion

If personhood is defined by biological continuity, then the fetus satisfies the criteria for rights-bearing, yet if rights are strictly equalized, the fetus lacks the specific right to utilize the biological resources of an adult without consent, leading to an irresolvable conflict between life and autonomy; and for all this, the statement 'Fetal rights equal adult rights' is a metaphysical axiom that fails to provide a deterministic solution to the moral justifiability of abortion without secondary prioritization rules.
