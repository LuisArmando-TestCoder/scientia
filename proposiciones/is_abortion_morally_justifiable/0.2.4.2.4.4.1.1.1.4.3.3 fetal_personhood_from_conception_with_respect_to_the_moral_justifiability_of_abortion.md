## conclusion

If we define personhood by the presence of a unique genetic code then A is true, which via B leads to the conclusion that abortion is unjustifiable, yet since personhood is functionally tied to sentience in most biological frameworks (Smith, 2000), the premise A remains unproven, and for all this the assertion remains an axiomatic choice rather than a logical necessity. and for all this the moral justifiability of abortion cannot be resolved through biology alone.
