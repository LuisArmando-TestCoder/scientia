## logical_tension

- **paradox**: The Self-Sustenance Paradox: A 'person' with full rights who cannot exist as a person without violating another person's fundamental rights.
- **ambiguity**: Does 'killing' (active) equal 'letting die' (passive withdrawal of support) in a gestational context?
- **useful_contradiction**: The fetus is 'one of us' yet 'inside of us', creating a unique ontological state where two individuals occupy the same physical space.

- Property Rights (Castle Doctrine): The right to remove an intruder from one's domain if they pose a threat to one's security/well-being.
