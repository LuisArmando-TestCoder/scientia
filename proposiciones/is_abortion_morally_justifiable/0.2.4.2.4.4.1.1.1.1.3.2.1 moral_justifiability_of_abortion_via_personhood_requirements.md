## logical_tension

- **paradox**: The Potentiality Paradox: A fetus is potentially a person, but to treat it as a person now is to grant rights based on a future state that may only exist because the rights were granted.

- **useful_contradiction**: The 'Sleep' contradiction: We protect sleeping persons who aren't self-aware, forcing us to redefine personhood as a 'persistent capacity' rather than a 'continuous act'.
