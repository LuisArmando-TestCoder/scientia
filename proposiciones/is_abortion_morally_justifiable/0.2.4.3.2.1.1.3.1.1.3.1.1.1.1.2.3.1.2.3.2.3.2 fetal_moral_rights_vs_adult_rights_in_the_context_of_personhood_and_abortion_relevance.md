## argument_reasons

- Identity Argument: A fetus is biologically a human and therefore possesses an inherent essence that grants it moral rights from the moment of conception.
- Continuity Argument: There is no non-arbitrary point in development where a 'non-person' becomes a 'person', thus personhood must be granted at the start.
- Potentiality Argument: A fetus possesses the roadmap to become a sentient adult, thus it should be treated with the rights of its future state.
