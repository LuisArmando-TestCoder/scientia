## central_semantic_node

Ontological and Moral Status of the Zygote/Fetus

## argument_reasons

- Biological continuity: The zygote is a genetically distinct living organism of the human species.
- Potentiality: The zygote possesses the internal teleology to develop into a sentient adult.
- Moral Equivalence: If personhood is based on species membership, it begins at conception.
- Absence of definitive transformation: There is no non-arbitrary point between conception and birth that creates 'personhood' where none existed.
