## logical_tension

- **paradox**: The Sovereign Substrate: To grant the fetus an 'absolute' right to life, one must negate the mother's 'absolute' right to her own body, meaning neither right is actually absolute.

- **useful_contradiction**: A person can have a right to life without having a right to the specific biological resources of another specific person to sustain that life.
